Our History

Our Journey of Excellence

A Legacy of Legal Excellence Since 2015

From our humble beginnings to becoming one of Malawi's most respected law firms, our journey has been marked by unwavering commitment to justice and client service.

2015

Foundation

Justice & Associates was established with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality legal services to the Malawian community.

2017

Expansion

Opened our second office in Lilongwe, expanding our reach and establishing specialized practice areas in corporate and family law.

2020

Innovation

Pioneered digital legal services in Malawi, introducing virtual consultations and online case management systems.

2023

Recognition

Named "Law Firm of the Year" by the Malawi Legal Excellence Awards, recognizing our commitment to legal excellence and community service.
